Why should I use friendlytransfer.com?

It is free for trust-based, friend-to-friend transactions.

You and your friends will save on international transfer fees.

You’ll have the chance to reconnect and build trust with friends around the world

How does it work?

You just follow these simple steps:

  1. Login via Facebook on our site

    • This allows us to use your Facebook information to (via our proprietary algorithm) identify transfer partners who you can potentially trust, based on common friendships and interests. Friendly Transfer does not share this information with third parties and does not post on Facebook on your behalf

  2. Improve your profile by providing additional information about you

    • The more information you provide on your profile, the more potential transaction partners you will be able to secure

  3. Propose a transaction on your dashboard
  4. Review the matching transactions provided back to you
  5. Choose the transactions that interest you the most and examine each transfer partner’s profile
  6. Contact the transfer partner you feel comfortable making a transaction with
  7. Agree on the specific terms of the transaction
  8. Once the the transaction has been completed, provide a review of your transfer partner. Reviews help build trust within our community
Is my information safe?

FriendlyTransfer does not share your social network information or completed transactions with third parties as stated in our Terms of Use.

Why do I have to join with Facebook?

Friendlytransfer.com leverages social networks to connect friends who need to transfer money. Friendlytransfer will never post on your behalf or share your social network information.
